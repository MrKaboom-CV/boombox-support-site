### What makes BooMBox different from traditional grading?
Unlike traditional grading, which relies on the subjective naked eye of a human grader, BooMBox uses an **advanced AI forensic toolkit**. Powered by the proprietary "Nano Banana" engine, it analyzes 4K high-resolution scans to detect surface micro-scratches, edge wear, and centering issues that are completely invisible to humans. Furthermore, it generates a full forensic report in under 3 seconds.

### What is "Tamper-Proof Vaulting"?
Tamper-proof vaulting ensures that a card's grade and forensic report can never be faked, swapped, or altered. 
1. **Digital Fingerprint:** The AI maps the microscopic fiber patterns and print dots of the physical card.
2. **Cryptographic Hash:** The 4K images, fingerprint, grade, and PDF report are compiled into a unique cryptographic hash.
3. **Blockchain Anchoring:** This hash is written to a public blockchain. If a bad actor tries to alter the PDF report or swap the physical card, the system will immediately flag it because the new data won't match the permanent, immutable blockchain record.

### What happens if someone scans the exact same card twice?
Because the AI generates a unique "Digital Fingerprint" based on the card's factory-level DNA (print dots, fiber structure), the system will immediately recognize if the card has been scanned before. If a user tries to resubmit a card two years later hoping for a better grade, BooMBox will flag it as already vaulted, and can even detect if new surface damage has occurred since the original scan!

### Is it legal to grade and track cards without a license from the manufacturers?
**Yes.** Thanks to the **First Sale Doctrine**, once a physical product (like a trading card) is sold by the manufacturer, they lose the right to control its secondary distribution. You are legally allowed to grade, vault, and trade the physical items you own. Additionally, under **Nominative Fair Use**, it is legally permissible to use names like "Charizard" or "Tom Brady" to accurately identify those items in our database. 
*(Note: BooMBox uses generic themes to avoid using trademarked logos without permission).*

### How does the Trade Binder work?
The Trade Binder is a highly customizable, theme-driven 9-pocket digital binder for peer-to-peer trading. Instead of exposing your entire vault of high-value assets to the public, you can move specific cards you are willing to trade into your Trade Binder. 

You can customize the look of the binder (e.g., carbon fiber, holographic foil, or generic team colors) and send secure invites to friends to propose and negotiate digital trades.

### Are my personal finances safe while developing BooMBox?
While bootstrapping a startup with personal funds is common, it is critical to maintain the "corporate veil." We highly recommend establishing a dedicated business bank account, using the "Transfer, Then Spend" method to fund the business account with personal capital, and keeping strict bookkeeping records of all startup costs.
