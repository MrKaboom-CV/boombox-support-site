## 1. Data Collection
When you use the **BooMBox Card Vault**, we collect data necessary to provide forensic analysis. This includes:
* **Image Data:** High-resolution scans processed by our **Nano Banana Engine**.
* **Account Info:** Email and username for vault access.
* **Blockchain Metadata:** Public wallet addresses used for anchoring forensic reports.

## 2. Nano Banana Engine Processing
Our AI engine analyzes surface texture and sub-surface integrity. This processing is done locally on our secure servers. We do not sell your card's raw image data to third-party marketplaces.

## 3. Data Retention
Forensic reports are stored indefinitely in your vault to ensure the "Tamper Proof" history of your collectible. You may request account deletion at any time via the Support Lab.
